How does it work?

You can purchase membership codes from us at a discounted rate to allow your employees, and even your favourite customers and clients, to get free access to the Legal Utopia app.

You can opt to purchase either an automatic application of “credits to each membership account to allow your members to redeem credits each time they purchase services or you can opt to purchase automatic subscriptions, this enables you to apply an annual subscription to your member’s account to gain full and unlimited access to all our services and content, including access to legal advice from our solicitors online.

Feedback Surveys

We give you insight on the aggregate usage and uptake of your members, how they rate the services and our app, as well as the evidence you need that your employees have legal problems and need legal support.

Building Authenticity

Retaining talented employees is interconnected with the authenticity of the relationship between employer and employee. It can only build with time.

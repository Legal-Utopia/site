Contract Review

 

Our contract review service is intended to be utilised as an on-demand support tool to assist a user in identifying a limited range of key or important clauses in a limited range of legal documents, contracts of insurance, or policy documents.

 

This service seeks to autonomously (without humans) scan a legal document that you upload or otherwise make available to Our system and label (with respect to the type of legal document it is). In doing this, We utilise Artificial Intelligence Technologies to locate clauses within the legal document based on the type of legal document you have made available. This is informed by a process We have developed based on information gathered by Us and third parties engaged by Us of similar or alike legal documents of that type.

 

This service may not be trained or proficient in your type of legal document that you make available to Our system, nor will Our Artificial Intelligence Technologies or process necessarily be able to identify or locate any clauses within any legal document type made available to it. If you make available to Our system a legal document that is within the scope of training or proficiency of Our Artificial Intelligence Technology, Our service may not be able to authoritatively assess, extract, classify or convert clauses within said legal document or produce an expected output.This is due to the use and limitation of Our Artificial Intelligence Technology and its potential for proficiency to vary substantially (it operates through an assessment of probabilities) and may still fail to assess, extract, classify or convert clauses with any degree of certainty.

 

This service will always seek the users’ upload of a legal document and shall rely on the users’ input of the document type, which shall be used to inform the process that the Artificial Intelligence will follow. It is therefore the users’ responsibility to upload the correct document type and correct document label.

 

This service has been designed to be used by informed laypersons who are over the age of 18, resident in England and Wales with a legal document or file subject to the jurisdiction and laws of England and Wales only. It is intended to be used for informative educational and general information purposes.

 

This service is not intended to be used for the purposes of, or in replacement of, professional legal advice or the services of regulated legal professionals. This service does not, and cannot, provide any definitive assessment or identification of the precise legal interpretation or meaning of any clauses within any legal documents, but that of a general assessment of clause variants.

 

Any “simple clause” conversions that are produced as a result of the use of this service are drafted on the basis of a collective interpretation of all clauses of that type or variant, including clauses of that legal document type that may be worded differently, of clauses within a limited range of training data. This forms the outer scope of the basis of the interpretation of any one clause and its subsequent “simple” wording conversion or explanation of meaning.

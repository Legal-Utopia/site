Frequently Asked Questions

This is amazing, how do I start a search?

When using the app service for the first time you will need to sign-up to create an account. You will then need to select a subscription to access our service.

1. Once you have created an account and signed-in, you will be presented with a welcome screen on the Search page. You can see which page you are on by look at the bottom navigation menu and you will see which page icon is highlighted in blue.

2. If you are on another page, go to the Search page. Once you are on the Search page, you will need to click on the slide-tab to active the "Start" button at the bottom of the screen. This tab is white and indicates your acceptance of the terms and conditions.

3. Once clicked, the start button will activate taking you to the next screen. You should now be on the Data Privacy screen and you will need to repeat your previous action. Click on the white slide-tab to active the blue "Continue" button.

4. Once clicked, you should be provided with a screen titled "Machine Learning" this gives you information on our use of technology. Click "Continue" to start the process.

5. You are now on the "Your Role" page. Select an option and then click on "Continue".

6. You are now on the "Status" page. Select an option and then click on "Continue".

7. You are now on the "Your Remedy" page. Select an option and then click on "Continue".

8. Ok, great. You should be presented with a text box. Please describe or explain your legal problem in this box; this must be 150 words long so be as descriptive as you can. Click on the "Continue" button once activated.

9. You will now be asked to confirm your selections from the previous pages. Click "Continue" to search.

10. You will now be presented with a range of questions to identify your problem. Follow the screen instructions as displayed.

What is included in the subscription?

We provide the Legal Utopia app to subscribers 24/7, 365 for you to identify your legal issue or dispute and the next steps to resolving it. This includes issuing legal guidance to educate you on the essential legal components to a particular circumstance, as well as provide directly relevant recommendations to resources, portals and services that can provide practical legal advice and support. The service will also notify you if your legal problem could be eligible for legal aid. Subscribers of the one month's subscription will be provided with unlimited use for one calendar month, at the end of the month you will need to re-subscribe. Subscribers of the annual subscription will be provided with unlimited use for one calendar year, at the end of the year you will need to re-subscribe. The subscription is inclusive, and no other fees are applied. Note that the services provided by other parties recommended to you are not included within the subscription and may come at additional cost.

Why is Machine Learning used?

Machine learning is a technology that is designed to be trained to complete tasks that would generally require a human to complete. Machine learning is a subfield of artificial intelligence. This service utilises natural language processing or NLP, which is a subfield of machine learning. NLP is a technology that can compute human languages and apply this understanding to a range of tasks that only humans could previously complete.We have put together an information page on the process of how we use artificial intelligence in our service, find out more at https://legalutopia.co.uk/machinelearning

When is Machine Learning used?

Machine learning is a developing technology that is utilised to provide our services to you, this has been utilised to categorise your description or explanation of your legal problem by statistical probability. It is only utilised when you start the search function and begin inputting information. The service does not use any personal data from your account to identify you.

How do you keep content up-to-date?

Legal Utopia works with third-parties, including law firms and information service providers, that provide updates to the content we display in-app.

Legal Utopia also monitors and reviews its contents database for changes to the law, as well as the availability, credibility and quality of legal services, portal and resource providers referred to in-app.

Where errors or updates to content are necessary, we accordingly act swiftly to make such changes.

Where possible, Legal Utopia will utilise third-party application programming interfaces (APIs) to update content.

In the event of you identifying errors or content updates, you can immediately notify our content review team via Customer Service. We aim to response within 12hrs.

Customer Service can be reached on our Contact Us page and by email at customerservice@legalutopia.co.uk.

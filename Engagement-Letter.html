Engagement Letter

Engagement with Legal Utopia Limited

Thank you seeking legal advice from Legal Utopia Limited

This engagement letter is provided to you following your selection of our firm to provide legal advice to you in respect of your legal issues on the platform provided by Legal Utopia Limited. This letter is to confirm the engagement of our firm and to give you certain information required by our professional regulatory body, the Solicitors Regulation Authority.

This engagement letter should be read in conjunction with the Terms of Sale between you and our Us, as well as the Confidentiality Agreement between you and Legal Utopia Limited. These documents form part of this engagement letter.

1. Scope of work

1.1. We will provide you with legal advice in relation to the general matters you’ve identified by you when booking your appointment with Us.

1.2. Any extension of work from this engagement will require you to re-book an appointment with us on the platform provided by Legal Utopia Limited.

1.3. We are only able to provide legal advice via the Legal Utopia Limited platform; we can accept instructions, subject to a separate engagement.

1.4. The acceptance of these terms will form our solicitor/client relationship, and our duty as your solicitor is owed to you, and you alone. Our legal advice is provided for the matter express by you via the Legal Utopia Limited platform only and is for the benefit of you alone. It should not be relied upon by third parties, including any directors and employees in their private capacity.

1.5. We shall undertake this engagement via audio or video-conferencing via Legal Utopia Limited.

1.6. Any additional requested or required correspondence will be provided via the abovementioned email; unless otherwise agreed.

2. Personnel

2.1. The solicitor you selected on our platform is the responsible fee earner for this engagement.

2.2. Other lawyers will not be engaged unless at your request or upon our advice to you and your express consent.

2.3. Robert Marcus shall be the supervising partner for the work undertaken and the primary point of contact. He can be contacted at

3. Costs

3.1. All our 30-minute calls are £37 per half hour, if you’ve purchased an inclusive subscription, you’ll have two of these calls included and these will be deducted from your account as they are used.

3.2. There is no obligation on you to take the engagement any further upon conclusion of the call.

3.3. Our half hourly rate after the conclusion of this call is subject to another £37 per half hour; unless you have existing call credits included within your subscription.

3.4. It is not expected that there will be any other disbursements or costs at this stage, should you engage us further this may change; however, we shall always seek your permission prior to incurring any additional costs.

4. Anti-money Laundering

4.1. We are required to undertake a Companies House search for any registered company we engage. If a shareholder holds 25% share of the entity, we will conduct an electronic check on that person(s) to verify their identity and their address, and if we are unable to do so, we may need to request more information from you.

4.2. Legal Utopia Limited may share personal information, upon consent, including any identification document and proof of residence document solely for the purposes of our legal obligations under clause 4.1.

5. Complaints

5.1. We always aim for the highest standards of service, but should there ever be a need to complain, you can find our complaints policy here. We will always aim to address your complaint informally to resolve the matter.

5.2. You have the right to make a complaint to the Legal Ombudsman here.

5.3. You have the right to make a complaint to the Solicitors Regulation Authority by submitting a report which can be found here.

Overview

We’re an inclusive social LawTech start-up on a mission to deliver access to law to everyone. We are not another platform copycat, we’re not here to deliver more of the same. We want to deliver something new, something disruptive, something with real impact on people.

If you’re looking to be part of a fast-paced and growing tech start-up in consumer legal services, fed-up with hearing more of the same, then maybe we’re a match! We’re still forming our culture and we want our new team to create this together, but we’ve got our goals and our mission, which we want your help to deliver.



Our Mission

Here at Legal Utopia, our mission is clear and simple: to make law affordable and accessible to everyone.



Equality, Diversity & Inclusion

We operate an Equal Opportunities and Diversity Policy and welcome applications from absolutely anyone and everyone.

Job Interviews

We’re not a stuffy firm. We’re a team of individuals working together towards a joint objective. If wearing a shirt and tie isn’t you, don’t wear one. We invite everyone coming to a job interview to wear what’s comfortable, what expresses who you are and what you enjoy.

We interview to get to know each other and we expect that first interviews take around 30 minutes with a senior staff member, regardless of the position. If we think you’re potentially a good fit, you may be invited back to another interview, this is to explore your skillset, attributes and qualifications in a more in-depth interview that may take upto 45 minutes. This will usually take place with a senior staff member and one other member you would be working with.



Things you should know

  • We’re flexible, yes you will still have deadlines and team meetings, but no you don’t have to work Mon-Fri or 9-5.
  • We’re virtual, yes we can get together in the office, but let’s not commute every day!
  • We’re tech focused and we like cloud services, we’re best friends with the cloud.

Ideal Candidate

When we’re recruiting we’re looking for the ideal candidate that can not only fulfil the job description but emerge and merge with our existing team, here’s our top six attributes:


  • Self-motivated, enthusiastic with a can do attitude
  • Bring innovative and unique approaches, express yourself
  • Communication and interpersonal skills, embody a good sense of team spirit
  • An active contributor willing to share ideas and ideals as an individual and team member
  • An individual that will support and lift their colleagues up whilst keeping honest and candid
  • A responsible, disciplined individual with the courage to say they got something wrong and they need help


Values

What we’ve come to need and expect from candidates is a combination of values that are reflective of the work we do and the way we do it. Here are the top six values we respect and desire:


  • Responsibility
  • Honesty
  • Courage
  • Creativity
  • Humility
  • Discipline


Testimonials




Career Opportunities

The world of work is not just about delivering for your employer but what your employer can deliver for you. We’re on an exciting path and that creates some awesome opportunities, projects and collaborations that help our employees’ careers excel.

Past employees have gone on to work for companies like: Macfarlanes, PriceWaterhouseCoopers, White & Case, Bramble, Stephenson Harwood, Al Tamimi & Company and Amazon to name a few


Innovation projects

Legal Utopia has been one of many technology start-ups leading the way on the application of machine learning to law, but we’re one of few to focus on people and not big business. We’ve been highly collaborative working with international law firms, regulators, universities, and government at both a national and local level.

Our collaborative approach has been highly successful in completing first-of-its-kind research into the application of supervised learning classifiers in unstructured, non-exhaustive training datasets with our method patent on legal corporus classifiers charging our solution forwards. We believe our collaborations have generated new market solutions that can impact the way people search, use, interact and consume legal services.


Network

Legal Utopia has built a modest network of alike thinkers but most importantly doers that report on their actions and not their intentions. We’ve grown as a business to value action over words, which means when we share our network with you, you’re connecting with a network that will do what they say they’re going to do and, with enthusiasm, achieve new things.


Legal Sector Collaborations

Legal Utopia has collaborated with, or been supported by, some of the biggest names in the legal world, including Baker McKenzie, Dentons, Norton Rose Fulbright, Mischon de Reya, and Travers Smith.

We’ve also taken a collaborative approach with UK regulators working with the Information Commissioner’s Office, Financial Conduct Authority, and Solicitors Regulation Authority.


Accelerators, Pilots, & Incubators

Legal Utopia has been recognised and accelerated by a range of high-profile accelerators, pilots, and incubators in the UK and US. We’ve been part of the Barclays LawTech Incubator, Wayra UK Accelerator, Google Digital Accelerator, London & Partners Programme, TechNation’s LawTech UK Sandbox, FCA Innovation Hub, SRA Customer Reviews Pilot and the Founders Embassy Accelerator.


Institutional & Government Support

Legal Utopia’s leading projects have been financially supported by some of the UK’s best known local and national government innovation funders including Innovate UK, The City of London Corporation, Department of Work & Pensions and Kent County Council, alongside funding programmes by the Treasury and the European Regional Development Fund.

Legal Utopia’s also received funding administered by Telefonica and the University of Edinburgh, as well as Anglia Ruskin University in addition to private investments from highly experienced lawyers, investors, and entrepreneurs.

This builds on the independent recognition of the different and progressive way in which Legal Utopia approaches utilising data and technology to drive new ways of delivering legal services in the UK. Joining Legal Utopia means being a part of progress and change and, most importantly, being recognised for it.
